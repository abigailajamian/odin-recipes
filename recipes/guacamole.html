A Mexican Avacado Dip

Ingredients

  • Avacados
  • Jalapeno
  • Lime
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut and dice all the vegtables
  2. Put 1/2 the lime,cilatro,jalapeno and onion in the molcajete
  3. Pound/crush the ingredients until it turns ito a paste like texture
  4. Add in the avacados, garlic powder, salt and pepper
  5. Cursh these in until you have the desired texture
  6. Add cilatro on top
