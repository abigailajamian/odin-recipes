Pasta

An Italian noodle dish

Ingredients

  • Penne Pasta
  • Roa's Vodka Sauce
  • Chicken Sausage
  • Onions
  • Bell Peppers
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut and dice all the vegtables
  2. Cut up chicken Sausage
  3. Boil water, add salt to water, and heat a seperate pan with vegtable oil in it
  4. Put the noodles in the boiling water for about 10 minutes, until tender
  5. Saute the chicken Sausage in the hot pan for about 5 minutes
  6. Remove chicken sausage and add all vegtables, saute for about 3 minutes, and chicken sausage back in
  7. Add the jar of vodka sauce
  8. Let it simmer until hot
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  10. Drain noodles, add them to your plate
  11. Put your pasts sauce on top
